ServiceNow offers a variety of modules that are divided into several categories:

  1. IT Operations Management (ITOM)
  2. IT Service Management (ITSM)
  3. Customer Service Management (CSM)
  4. Security Operations (SECOPS)
  5. Human Resources
  6. Project Portfolio Management (PPM)
  7. Financial Management (FM)
  8. Performance Analytics (PA)
  9. IT Business Management (ITBM)

Each of these modules has a range of features and capabilities designed to meet the specific needs of different departments and industries.

The IT Service Management (ITSM) module in ServiceNow is a comprehensive solution for managing IT services, processes, and operations. Some of the key features and capabilities of the ITSM module include:

  1. Incident Management: This helps IT teams track, prioritize, and resolve incidents and service requests in a timely manner.
  2. Problem Management: This allows IT teams to identify, isolate, and resolve underlying problems that are causing incidents.
  3. Change Management: This helps IT teams plan, assess, and approve changes to IT systems, applications, and infrastructure to minimize downtime and reduce the risk of incidents.
  4. Configuration Management: This provides a centralized repository for IT configuration items (CIs) and enables IT teams to track and manage the relationships between CIs.
  5. Service Catalog: This allows IT teams to create and manage a catalog of services that can be requested by users, and automate the fulfillment of service requests.
  6. Knowledge Management: This helps IT teams create and share knowledge articles, standard operating procedures, and best practices with users.
  7. Asset Management: This provides a centralized repository for IT assets and enables IT teams to track and manage the lifecycle of assets, including procurement, deployment, maintenance, and retirement.

The ITSM module in ServiceNow is designed to be highly customizable and can be tailored to meet the specific needs and requirements of different organizations.
